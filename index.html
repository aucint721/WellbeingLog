About Wellbeing Log

Wellbeing Log is a comprehensive iOS app designed for schools, counselors, and administrators who need to monitor student attendance and wellbeing across different support rooms. The app provides real-time tracking, data management, and detailed reporting capabilities.

How to Use

  1. Start Your Trial: Download the app and enjoy 30 days of full access
  2. Student In: Select a room, choose a student, and record their entry with a reason
  3. Student Out: Find students currently in a room and record their exit with an outcome
  4. Head Count: View current student counts and lists for each room
  5. Statistics: Access detailed reports on student movements and room usage
  6. Settings: Manage student data, import/export CSV files, and customize reasons
  7. Upgrade to Premium: When ready, easily upgrade to unlock all features permanently

Privacy & Security

Wellbeing Log prioritizes your privacy and security:

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored locally on your device
  • No Data Transmission: No information is sent to external servers

  • Complete Control: You have full control over your data
