Student Room Management System

About Wellbeing Log

Wellbeing Log is a comprehensive school management app designed to track student wellbeing and manage room-based student logging. Perfect for schools, counselors, and administrators who need to monitor student attendance and wellbeing across different support rooms.

Key Features

Student Tracking

Track students entering and exiting different rooms (Wellbeing, Diverse Learners, Lunch) with customizable reasons and timestamps.

Real-time Headcount

Monitor current student counts in each room with live updates and detailed statistics.

Data Management

Import and export student data via CSV files, with comprehensive backup and restore functionality.

Statistics & Reports

Generate detailed reports on student movements, room usage, and wellbeing trends.

Customizable Reasons

Set up custom reasons for student entries and exits to match your school's specific needs.

iPad & iPhone Optimized

Fully optimized interface for both iPad and iPhone with responsive design and intuitive navigation.

How to Use

  1. Student In: Select a room, choose a student, and record their entry with a reason.
  2. Student Out: Find students currently in a room and record their exit with an outcome.
  3. Head Count: View current student counts and lists for each room.
  4. Statistics: Access detailed reports on student movements and room usage.
  5. Settings: Manage student data, import/export CSV files, and customize reasons.

Privacy & Security

Wellbeing Log prioritizes your privacy and security:

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored locally on your device
  • No Data Transmission: No information is sent to external servers
  • No Tracking: No analytics or tracking data is collected
  • Complete Control: You have full control over your data

Common Questions

Q: How do I import student data?
A: Go to Settings → Student Management → Import Students and select a CSV file.

Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes! Go to Settings → Student Management → Export Students to save your data.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

System Requirements

  • iOS Version: iOS 18.5 or later
  • Devices: iPhone and iPad
  • Storage: 50 MB available space
  • Permissions: File access for CSV import/export
