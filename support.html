Common Issues & Solutions

  • iCloud Disconnected: Check iCloud sign-in and internet connection in Settings
  • Sync Failed: Verify CloudKit permissions and iCloud storage availability
  • No Updates: Ensure all devices are signed into the same iCloud account
  • Data Not Syncing: Check internet connection and CloudKit Dashboard setup
  • App Crashes: Update to latest iOS version and restart the app
  • Permission Errors: Grant necessary permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security
